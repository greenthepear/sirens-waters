Aranya is the first exoplanet colonized by humans. It orbits Aranya Alpha within a star system of the same name among 4 other planets, located 15.2 light years from the Solar system. It has a circumference around 1.1 times that of Earth, and is orbited by two moons: Laika and Baker.
Aranya's surface is about 51% covered in salt water oceans, the vast majority of land being a lush jungle with alien fauna, flora and funga, all three known to be dangerous to humans. The composition of aranyan soil and airborne micro-organisms make it impossible for human agriculture in non-specialized envoriments.
Although the planet was discovered by the James Webb Telescope on the 12th of May 2025, research interest greatly increased after a strong seemingly structured radio signal was detected from the direction of the star system on the 6th of June 2027, now know as Siren's song.
First successful unmanned flyby mission finished in 2098, the first orbiter and lander in 2110 and 2120 respectfully. Spaceflight to Aranya increased drastically after the invention of the Superluminal in 2122, allowing for faster-than-light travel. After an international effort, the first permanent manned orbital space station 'Bridge' made it possible for humans to make landfall in 2131. In 2137 and 2142 respectfully, two bigger space stations were created - 'Treehouse' and 'Crown', by the time of which human settlement on the Horn Peninsula reached a million people, with the support of orbital farming on the aforementioned space stations.
After the Awakening, space stations no longer exist in aranyan orbit, and the estimated 120k people on the planet are fully isolated from any type of communication with Earth.
