General Colonial Government

General Colonial Government, often shortened to GCG is the largest political entity on Anda and the successor to the sole governing body during the colonization of the Horn Peninsula before the Awakening, then called Colonial Command.

Government

The government of the GCG consists of remnants of various officials of the Colonial Command as well as its structure which used resemble more of a project committee than a government of a state, with important decisions coming from Earth command despite extreme delays in communication thanks to distance. After the Awakening and Earth command inaccessible, panic ensued and the inefficiency of this arrangement became bluntly apparent. Still it took years of navigating beurocratic gridlock to reform the Colonial Command into the GCG.

The reformed government can be best described as an oligarchy with the main governing body called the Colonial Committee consisting of 80 members called Colonial Governors or just Governors. The Governors are a mix of old CC officials, military personnel, elected local administrators and generally influential people like business owners and family members of other Governors. The main qualification needed to become a Governor seems to be seniority, as Governors have an average age of 75 with the main reason for seat vacancy being death from old age. The Committee is regularly accused of corruption but younger members especially so.

Despite the overarching oligarchic system, an increasing number of local administrators, especially mayors, are elected democratically. Demonstrations in support of democracy are commonplace.
