Andan Nuclear Authority

Andan Nuclear Authority, or ANA is an organization controlling the territory around the Pandit Research Center on the Horn Peninsula. Originally the main scientific institute for the Colonial Command, it is now the only known body to possess (thermo)nuclear warheads. As a country, it can be described as an extremely isolationist technocracy, whose independence threads on nuclear deterrence.

History

The ANA practically exists as a sovereign state because of the Famished Uprising, during which the CC tried to force the Pandit Research Center to repurpose civilian nuclear research departments as warhead factories to use against the bostonian rebels. An unknown number of warheads were actually created however the scientists refused to hand them over to the military, as most of them sympathized with the uprising. When an inexperienced military force was sent in to muscle the handover, a messy battle erupted at the PRA, with the scientists coming in victorious thanks to improvised weaponry and knowledge of the research center layout. Suspecting an another force was coming, the ANA detonated a minor nuclear bomb on one of the jungle roads leading to the PRA from the CC capital. The idea was for the detonation to happen in an isolated spot, mostly harming the wildlife, but many cases of radiation sickness were reported in both ANA and CC territories. The scientists threatened the CC to not enter within a radius of the PRA or further nuclear attacks will be launched on military and civilian targets, claiming they posses a thousand more bombs. Despite their original sympathy to the bostonians, nuclear threats are still targeted on the Free Anda Republic and virtually any other foreign entity.
